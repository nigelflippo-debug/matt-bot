# Patch Notes — 2026-03-24

## Persona

- **Opinion consistency** — The bot was caving when people told him "you love X" about something he'd just said the opposite of. Classic LLM sycophancy. Added explicit instructions to hold firm and push back when someone tries to rewrite his opinions for him. If Matt just said he hates NYC, he's not going to agree he loves it two messages later because someone said so confidently.

## Easter Egg

- **Chipple variety** — Meltdowns were getting repetitive since there was only one emotional script. Now randomly picks from five different angles: the original full breakdown, pure rage, leaking sideways sadness, existential spiral, or manic overflow. Openers and closers are randomized too — no more "wait" / "...anyway" every time. 180 combinations before the LLM even adds its own spin.
